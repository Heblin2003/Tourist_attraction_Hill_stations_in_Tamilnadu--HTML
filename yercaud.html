YERCAUD:  Yercaud is a picturesque hill station located in 
the Shevaroy Hills of the Eastern Ghats in Tamil Nadu, India.Situated 
at an altitude of about 1,500 meters above sea level, Yercaud is 
known for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and pleasant climate 
throughout the year. The town is surrounded by coffee plantations,
orange groves,and spice gardens, creating a refreshing and 
tranquil atmosphere.

TOP ATTRACTION IN YERCAUD

The most alluring aspect of Yercaud is its big yet natural lake very popularly known as the Emerald Lake. Surrounded by some wonderful cloud-peaked hills and a well persevered garden on its banks, this lake is a feast for the eyes.

Lady's Seat a viewpoint that overlooks the winding ghat road and offers a spectacular view of Mettur Dam and Salem town. The place also houses a viewing tower, which is equipped with a telescope to enjoy the beautiful view.

Located in the eastern part of the Yercaud Hills, Pagoda point is a beautiful viewpoint where one is treated to a panoramic view of the entire town of Salem as well as the neighbouring village of Kakambadi.

The Kiliyur Falls drops from a height of 300 feet and are a popular tourist attraction near Salem.They also offer boating and swimming activities in their vicinity.These waterfalls are a part of the Shervaroyan hill in Eastern Ghats.

Servarayan Temple(Shevaroy Temple) The cave has idol of Lord Servaryan and Goddess Kaveri. The Servarayan hills are named after the Lord and Goddess Kaveri represent the river Kaveri.

Anna Park is a well preserved garden and is a combination of four small parks known as Lake Park, Deer Park, Gandhi Park and Children's Park. The park houses a wide collection of natural plants and trees around Shevaroy hills.

The Silk Farm and Rose Garden is situated at a distance of 2m from Yercaud. It is a famous sightseeing location in Yercaud. It is a perfect location wherein families can learn about the techniques of silk production .

A must see for any tourist visiting Yercaud, The Bear's cave is situated in a private coffee estate near the Norton Bungalow, It was named the bear's cave because the caves were believed to house bears.

A renowned educational institution known for its colonial architecture and beautiful campus.>Montfort

The Grange was built in the year 1820 CE by a British collector M.D. Cockburn, who is well known as the father of Yercaud. This mansion is one of the oldest architectural monuments in Yercaud.

An ideal location for get-togethers and picnics, Satmalia Deer Park is a famous tourist stopover in Silvassa. The park lies in close proximity to the Vasona Lion Safari Park and is home to many species of deer and antelopes.

Karadiyur, The mesmerizing view from the Karadiyur viewpoint is one of the prime attractions in Yercaud. The view of sunrise as well as sunset from this location is a unique attraction.

THINGS TO SEE IN YERCAUD

Enjoy the scenic beauty from Tipperary Viewpoint, which offers panoramic views of the lush valleys, coffee plantations, and the Eastern Ghats. It's a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers.Tipperary View Point is placed at the southernmost give up of Yercaud, and you could reach her by means of taking the Tipperary Road.

Gents Seat is another viewpoint offering stunning vistas of the surrounding hills and valleys. It is a peaceful place to soak in the beauty of the landscape and enjoy a calm and serene atmosphere.Gent's Seat is a wonderful viewpoint in Yercaud town close to Lady's Seat.The Gent's Seat is one of the prime attractions of Yercaud .

While in Yercaud, don't miss trying the local cuisine. Sample traditional South Indian dishes like dosa, idli, and vada from local eateries. You can also try the unique flavors of Yercaud's filter coffee, a specialty in the region

Explore Norton's Bungalow, a historic colonial building dating back to the 1800s. This heritage bungalow is known for its architectural charm and picturesque setting amidst lush greenery. Visitors can appreciate the old-world charm of the bungalow and enjoy the tranquil surroundings.
